AN INTRODUCTION TO ARTIFICIAL INTELLIGENCE

Getting started with Artificial Intelligence can lead to numerous questions and confusion, given the speed the world is changing and adopting this technology. There are plenty of resources available online but there has to be a start point. In this article, there is a brief introduction of Artificial Intelligence covering all its important aspects which one must go through to get a clear picture of this emerging technology. Artificial Intelligence has made commendable progress and is developing at a lightning-fast speed covering every industry of a market. AI has become a necessity rather than an extra activity to know about this technology and its evolving faces.
